# IvaBot

> AI SEO tools for small business, with real Google data

**[ivabot.xyz](https://ivabot.xyz)** · [About](https://ivabot.xyz/about) · [Try it free](https://ivabot.xyz/app)

IvaBot is an AI-powered SEO chatbot that combines real-time Google SERP data with 15+ years of SEO expertise. Three tools in one chat, designed for small businesses, solo founders, and content creators who want SEO that works without the complexity or the price.

## What it does

- **🔍 Core Audit** — Full technical SEO audit. Tells you exactly what's holding your page back from ranking.
- **✍️ Content Builder** — Writes briefs and articles based on live SERP data, with the keywords and structure your competitors are using.
- **📊 Content Coverage** — Shows what your page is missing compared to top-ranking pages, plus AI Readiness for ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews.

## Why it's different

Most AI SEO tools generate content from patterns or wrap existing platforms in a chat interface. IvaBot does neither.

- Pulls **live SERP data** from DataForSEO at the moment of each request
- Parses your **actual page** content and structure
- Uses prompts grounded in 15+ years of real SEO judgment
- **Pay-as-you-go** pricing with a free tier — no subscription, no monthly minimums

## Who it's for

- Small business owners who want to rank but can't afford agencies
- Solo founders who need SEO that doesn't require a marketing degree
- Content creators who want briefs and articles backed by real data
- Marketing teams looking for a focused tool without the bloat

## Stack

Built solo by a non-developer using AI as a collaborator:

- Frontend: Webflow + custom React components
- Backend: Supabase (auth, database, Edge Functions, storage)
- AI: GPT-4o-mini for content generation
- Data: DataForSEO API for live SERP, keyword volume, ranked keywords
- Payments: Stripe (pay-as-you-go credits)
